WODBrain Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-11-13

Overview

WODBrain helps you scan and manage workouts. We respect your privacy and collect only what is needed to operate the app and provide premium features. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your Firebase User ID (UID) and email address.
  • Purchases & subscriptions: We receive subscription status and related metadata from RevenueCat. We do not store full payment details.
  • Usage metrics: We count OCR and AI extraction requests and basic service usage to enforce free-tier limits and improve reliability.
  • Content you submit: Images you send for OCR are transmitted to our server and processed by Google Cloud Vision to return text. If you opt to use AI plan extraction, the extracted workout text (not the image) may be sent to our AI provider to structure it into a timer plan.
  • Device & logs: Basic diagnostic logs (e.g., timestamps, request identifiers, status codes, error traces). In some cases we may log short, truncated previews of submitted text to help debug failures; we minimize this and avoid logging image content.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and maintain accounts.
  • Provide premium features and verify entitlements.
  • Process images via Google Cloud Vision to extract text.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the app and backend services.
  • Enforce fair-use limits for free tiers.
  • Optionally structure extracted workout text into a timer plan via our AI provider when enabled.

Sharing with Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and Firestore data storage.
  • Google Cloud Vision: OCR of images you submit.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and entitlement status.
  • OpenAI: When AI plan extraction is enabled, we send extracted workout text (no images) to OpenAI to convert it into a structured timer plan.

We do not sell your personal data.

Data Retention

  • Account and subscription records: Retained while your account exists.
  • Usage counters: Retained for monthly quota tracking and audits.
  • Images: Transmitted for processing and not stored long-term beyond what is necessary to fulfill the request and operate logs.
  • Logs: Retained for a limited period to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service, then deleted or aggregated.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. Provider systems (Firebase/Google Cloud, RevenueCat, and OpenAI) use encryption at rest. We use access controls and least-privilege credentials for backend services. We do not implement end-to-end encryption for user-submitted images or text; data is protected in transit and at rest by our infrastructure and service providers.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You may sign out at any time.
  • To request deletion of account-associated data, contact us using the email below.
  • You can choose not to use AI plan extraction; OCR-only functionality will remain available subject to your plan.
